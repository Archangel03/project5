In search of the most beautiful parts of the world, we often do not notice how beautiful cities are in Ukraine. It is not for nothing that they are often mentioned in poetry by writers from all over the world, depicted on canvases and sung in songs. What Ukrainian places are able to surprise with their beauty and architecture, cause admiration and desire to come to them again?

Vinnytsia is a part of European tranquility

Would you like to see a modern European town without leaving Ukraine? Then you should come to Vinnytsia! This is an incredibly beautiful city located on the picturesque banks of the South Bug River. Thanks to the well-preserved monuments of the 14th century.
