In search of the most beautiful parts of the world, we often do not notice how beautiful cities are in Ukraine. It is not for nothing that they are often mentioned in poetry by writers from all over the world, depicted on canvases and sung in songs. What Ukrainian places are able to surprise with their beauty and architecture, cause admiration and desire to come to them again?

UMAN IS A FLOWER OF GREENNESS AND ROMANCE

One of the most beautiful places in Ukraine is romantic Uman, where there are always many tourists thanks to one of the largest arboretums - Sofiivka. In addition, the city is known for the "Pearl of Love" - a light and musical fountain, which is one of the three largest fountains in Ukraine. It has become one of the favorite vacation spots not only for lovers, but also for tourists.
